What We Do For You

We take ownership of your procurement-to-asset lifecycle — backed by our purpose-built platform to deliver results from day one.

Procurement Governance

We design multi-level approval workflows matched to your authority structure. Approvals adapt to department, category, and amount — no blanket sign-offs, no bottlenecks.

Asset Register & Lifecycle

We maintain a living asset register that updates as assets are procured, moved, written off, or disposed. Depreciation, year-end snapshots, and reporting — all handled.

Goods Receipt & Mismatch Resolution

Our solution covers physical gate entry, goods receipt verification, and invoice mismatch detection. Quantity or price discrepancies are flagged before payment — preventing leakage.

Physical Verification

We provide solutions for QR-tagged identification, phone-based scanning, and periodic verification campaigns. Know what you have, where it is, and what condition it's in.

Vendor & Contract Management

We manage your vendor lifecycle — onboarding, compliance tracking, AMC and warranty monitoring, and service records. All in one place, with alerts before contracts lapse.

ERP & Accounting Integration

Our solution enables seamless integration with Tally out of the box. Need SAP, QuickBooks, or another ERP? We configure it for you. Vouchers and asset masters flow automatically.
