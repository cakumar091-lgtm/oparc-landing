Multi-Level Approvals

Configurable approval matrix with line-level or collective topology. Supports 2-phase MOVEMENT approvals across departments.

GST & TDS Compliant

CGST/SGST/IGST auto-split, HSN/SAC master, TDS at source, ITC tracking. Ready for Indian accounting standards.

Full Procurement Cycle

PR → PO → Gate Entry → GRN → Invoice Matching → Asset Register. Every step tracked and audited.

QR-Based Asset Tagging

Generate tags, print QR labels, scan with any phone camera. Physical verification campaigns with discrepancy tracking.

Depreciation & Reporting

SLM/WDV depreciation with year-end snapshots. Asset register, expense reports, and CSV exports.

Multi-Tenant & Secure

Each organization's data is fully isolated. Role-based access, JWT authentication, and complete audit trails.

Tally Integration

Auto-generate Tally XML vouchers for journal entries, purchase vouchers, and asset masters. Configurable ledger mapping.

Vendor Debit Notes

Raise debit notes for short supply, damage, price mismatch, or quality issues. PDF generation with GST breakdowns.
